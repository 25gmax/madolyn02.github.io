STEM

Involvement with FIRST:

I am a programming and electrical captain for my school's FIRST robotics team, 5216 the E-Ville Empire, which is definitely teaching me some valuable skills that will help my future career. Both years I have been on the team we have qualified for both the Michigan State Championship and the Detroit World Championship. I've also contributed to FIRST in my community in other ways such as mentoring the junior high FIRST Tech Challenge team, team 14623 E-Ville Bounty Hunters and the FIRST LEGO League teams during the summer at our local Boys and Girls Club and during their season.

Computer Science:

I have been learning to code since sixth grade when my Tech 6 teacher introduced our class to it. I immediately became interested and haven't stopped learning since. Since then, I've taken Computer Programming and Design and Computer Lit, and AP Computer Science at my high school. In my Programming and Design class, I have picked up on some of the basics of various programming languages including Java, JavaScript, Python. I also learned HTML and CSS. In 2018 from January to May, I mentored and facilitated a coding club at my local junior high school. In June 2018, I attended a STEM Scholars Network Coding and Networking Camp at Saginaw Valley State University and learned various things about networking such as how to set up a LAN network, make my own Ethernet cord. I aspire to go to college and combine my love for computer science with some form of data or laboratory science one day. My ultimate goal is to use my skills to help people in need.
